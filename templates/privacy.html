Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering and search. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, and you use Spendly at your own risk.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond operating the service. We do not currently integrate with third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
